# Flow Timer - ADHD-Friendly Pomodoro

> A responsive web-based timer application designed specifically for people with ADHD, supporting flow states rather than interrupting them.

This application was created to provide a more flexible pomodoro timer that recognizes when users are in productive flow states and allows them to continue working without disruption.
